Noriko

Personal project

Recently, I discovered an amazing short animation series named Gunbuster, and I instantly got hooked on the main character, Noriko. She inspired me to draw an illustration, which then gave me the idea to sculpt her in 3D. It's been a while since I've worked on a human character, so I felt a bit rusty, but I thought it would be the perfect opportunity to challenge myself and start a new project.

And there it is! I really enjoyed diving into the process of creating an entire human character from scratch again, and I decided to use Blender for this project, even though I had almost zero knowledge of the software. I have to admit, I've fallen in love with Blender! It's such a powerful software!
